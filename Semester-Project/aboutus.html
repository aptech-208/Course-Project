The Richfield Standard

Our Story

Richfield Chairs began with a simple observation: the world is full of chairs, but very few truly understand the human form.

Every curve, every stitch, and every material choice is made with the intention of providing lasting support for those who dream, work, and rest in our seats.

Why Richfield?

Premium Materials

Sustainably sourced hardwoods and top-grain Italian leathers for ultimate longevity.

Ergonomic Science

Orthopedic standards ensure your spine is perfectly supported throughout the day.

Timeless Design

A Richfield chair is designed to look as modern in twenty years as it does today.

Our Mission

We believe that luxury shouldn't cost the planet. Richfield is committed to a carbon-neutral manufacturing process by 2030.

We use recycled alloys and ensure that for every tree used in our frames, two more are planted in its place.
